Format Rules Banned Cards
Standard A constructed format where the following sets are legal:
 • VCL, MSS, DRT, FES, POS, INF, PSB, BLZ, NGA, RST 		No bans
Primordial A constructed format where all cards in a deck must come from the same expansion. Any expansion that has passed through standard is legal, i.e:
 • KFS, KOF, CRO, ABY, WOG, AZU, SKA, EXS, NAT, VCL, MSS, DRT, FES, POS, INF, PSB, BLZ, NGA, RST 		No bans
Legacy A constructed format where the following sets are legal:
 • KFS, KOF, CRO, CCRO, ABY, CABY, WOG, GEX, CWOG, AZU, AZA, SKA, CSKA, EXS, NAT, VCL, MSS, DRT, FES, POS, INF, PSB, BLZ, NGA, RST, CTMW, PMW 		No bans
Pauper A constructed format using the same card list as legacy, but with the additional restriction that only cards printed at common are allowed. No bans
Commander A singleton format where you choose a legendary creature or vehicle to be your commander. It has the same card legality as legacy, however all cards in your deck must have their color identity be a subset of you commander's color identity. Wheel of Woe
