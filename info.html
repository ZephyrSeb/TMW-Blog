Search Tips and Syntax

The search includes a number of expressions you can use to filter cards, as follows.

Expression

Description

set:

Searches for cards from the corresponding set.

c:, color:

Searches by color (W, U, B, R, or G). Will try to match all colors listed.

ci:

Searches by color identity (W, U, B, R, or G). Will try to match all colors listed.

t:, type:

Searches for cards with the corresponding type, subtype, or supertype.

o:

Searches the card's textbox for the corresponding text.

mv:

Searches for cards with the corresponding mana value.

p:, pow:

Searches for cards with the corresponding power.

tou:

Searches for cards with the corresponding toughness.

r:, rarity:

Searches for cards with the corresponding rarity.

is:

Searches for cards with the corresponding property. currently, you can check for is:permanent, is:historic, is:dfc, is:split, or is:vanilla.

For search terms that search by color or number, you can also use inequalities to find all cards that meet the inequality.

To include multiple search terms, separate them with a space.
